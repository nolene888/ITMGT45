About Us

Digital Espresso is a cozy local cafe that started in 2025. Rooted in a passion for great coffee and a love for community, we take pride in serving handcrafted drinks made from quality, locally sourced ingredients. More than just a cafe, we are committed to sustainability -- minimizing waste, supporting local farmers, and creating an eco-friendly space where customers can enjoy their coffee-guilt free.
